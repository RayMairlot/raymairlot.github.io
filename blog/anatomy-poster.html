Needed an anatomy poster to go on Fester's bedroom wall as part of my Addams Family House project:

It was quite fun and I probably went into a bit more detail that necessary so I'll probably simplify it a bit later and make it look a bit more 2D.

When I posted it on Reddit I did have some mild accusations that maybe it was AI or I'd used AI for the concept. For the record, I did not and I am against using Generative AI. The poster was based on a real 'Organs of the Body' poster from Amazon and the style I did it in is consistent with the style I have been using for the other props I've made.
